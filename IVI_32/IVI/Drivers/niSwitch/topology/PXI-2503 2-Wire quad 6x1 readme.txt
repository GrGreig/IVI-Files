The "PXI-2503 2-Wire quad 6x1.top" file is used to configure the PXI-2503 as four independant 6x1 two-wire multiplexers.  Channels 0-5 will connect to common0, channels 6-11 will connect to common1, channels 12-17 will connect to common2, and channels 18-23 will connect to common3.  Configuration of the PXI-2503 can be done in T&M explorer, or using the init_with_options function.
